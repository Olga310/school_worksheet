My School Day

Hi! My name is Emma, and I want to tell you about my school day. I study many interesting subjects that help me learn about the world.

In the morning, I have Maths. I use my calculator and notebook to solve problems with numbers. Maths started in ancient Egypt over 4,000 years ago! After Maths, I have History. My teacher shows us old photos and tells stories about people from long ago. We learn about kings, wars, and how people lived before us.

My favorite lesson is Geography. I love looking at the map on the wall and finding different countries. We also have a big globe on the teacher's desk. It shows our planet Earth! The teacher says Geography helps us understand our world better.

On Tuesdays, we have PE (Physical Education). I always wear my white trainers and sports clothes. We play football, run, and do exercises. PE keeps our bodies healthy and strong.

Art class is very creative. We use scissors to cut paper and make beautiful pictures. Some students paint, and others draw. Art helps us show our ideas and feelings.

In Science class, we learn about animals, plants, and how things work. We do fun experiments with water and plants. Sometimes we use the laptop to watch videos about space and dinosaurs.

Music lessons are loud but fun! We sing songs and play instruments. Music exists in every country around the world.

When I don't understand a word, I use my English dictionary. It helps me find meanings of new words.

School subjects and objects help me learn new things every day. What is your favorite subject?
